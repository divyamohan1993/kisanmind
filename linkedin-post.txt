We built KisanMind for the ET GenAI Hackathon 2026.

The problem: 150M Indian farmers make decisions worth Rs 45 lakh crore annually — without seeing satellite data, without comparing mandi profits, without crop-specific weather actions, and mostly not in their language.

What we built: One phone call. Farmer says their crop in any of 22 Indian languages. In 30 seconds, they get:

- Crop health from 4 satellites (Sentinel-2, SAR, MODIS, NASA SMAP)
- Best mandi ranked by NET profit (price minus transport, commission, spoilage)
- Date-specific weather actions ("harvest before Friday's rain")
- Cross-validated: if satellites say soil is wet but crop is declining, we flag pest — not drought

The math: +26% income per farmer. Rs 29,000/year.
At 100K farmers: Rs 2.9 billion in value created.

9 real data sources. Zero fake data. Every number from a verified API.
