TITLE:
KisanMind — One Phone Call, Four Satellites, 22 Languages | ET GenAI Hackathon 2026

DESCRIPTION:
KisanMind delivers satellite crop health, live mandi prices, and weather-timed farming advice to Indian farmers — via a single voice call in 22 languages.

Live demo: A farmer calls in Hindi, says "bhindi in Solan" — and gets personalized advice from 4 satellites, 112 crop prices, and 5-day weather in 30 seconds.

What happens in those 30 seconds:
0:00 - Farmer speaks crop name in Hindi
0:05 - Gemini 3 Flash extracts intent
0:08 - 9 APIs fire in parallel (4 satellites + mandi + weather + maps)
0:25 - Cross-validation catches contradictions
0:28 - Advisory synthesized and spoken back via TTS
0:30 - Beep + voice advisory delivered

Data sources (all real, zero fake):
- Sentinel-2 — NDVI/EVI/NDWI crop health (10m resolution)
- Sentinel-1 SAR — Radar soil moisture (works through clouds)
- MODIS Terra — Land surface temperature / heat stress (1km daily)
- NASA SMAP — Root-zone moisture 0-100cm deep (9km)
- AgMarkNet — 112 crop prices from data.gov.in
- Open-Meteo — 5-day forecast + 90-day historical for growth stage
- Google Maps — Real driving distances to every mandi
- Gemini 3 Flash — Advisory synthesis with 5-model fallback

Impact: +26% income per farmer (Rs 29,000/year)
