# Common Crop Pests and Diseases — Identification Guide

## Tomato

### Late Blight (Phytophthora infestans)
- **Symptoms**: Dark brown/black lesions on leaves, white fuzzy growth underneath
- **Conditions**: Cool (15-20°C), wet weather, >85% humidity
- **Impact**: Can destroy entire crop in 7-10 days if untreated
- **Action**: Remove infected plants immediately. Consult local KVK for treatment.

### Fruit Borer (Helicoverpa armigera)
- **Symptoms**: Round holes in fruits with frass (insect excrement)
- **Conditions**: Warm weather, more common in kharif season
- **Action**: Install pheromone traps. Consult local KVK for biological control options.

## Wheat

### Yellow Rust (Puccinia striiformis)
- **Symptoms**: Yellow-orange stripes on leaves
- **Conditions**: Cool (10-15°C), humid weather
- **Impact**: Can reduce yield 20-40% if untreated
- **Action**: Report to nearest agriculture office. Consult KVK for fungicide recommendation.

### Aphids
- **Symptoms**: Small green insects on leaves and ears, honeydew on surfaces
- **Conditions**: Warm, dry spells in Feb-March
- **Action**: Natural predators (ladybugs) often control. Consult KVK if severe.

## Rice

### Blast (Magnaporthe oryzae)
- **Symptoms**: Diamond-shaped lesions on leaves, neck rot
- **Conditions**: Cool nights, warm days, high humidity
- **Action**: Use resistant varieties. Consult KVK for management.

### Brown Plant Hopper (BPH)
- **Symptoms**: Hopper burn — plants dry in circular patches
- **Conditions**: High nitrogen use, standing water
- **Action**: Drain fields for 3-4 days. Avoid excessive nitrogen. Consult KVK.
