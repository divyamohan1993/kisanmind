# PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)

## Overview
Direct income support of ₹6,000 per year to farmer families, paid in 3 installments of ₹2,000 each.

## Eligibility
- All landholding farmer families across India
- Subject to certain exclusion criteria (institutional landholders, income tax payers, etc.)

## How to Apply
1. Visit nearest Common Service Centre (CSC)
2. Or apply online at pmkisan.gov.in
3. Required documents: Aadhaar card, bank account details, land records

## Payment Schedule
- Installment 1: April-July (��2,000)
- Installment 2: August-November (₹2,000)
- Installment 3: December-March (₹2,000)

## Check Status
- Visit: pmkisan.gov.in → Beneficiary Status
- Enter Aadhaar number or mobile number

## Other Key Schemes
- **PMFBY** (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana): Crop insurance at 2% premium for kharif, 1.5% for rabi
- **KCC** (Kisan Credit Card): Short-term crop loans at 4% interest (with prompt repayment)
- **Soil Health Card**: Free soil testing and nutrient recommendations
